Project Overview: Käkedom Tools

Our initiative focuses on empowering refugee farmers in Kiryandongo Refugee Camp through a holistic approach

Our Comprehensive Approach

Smart IoT Technology

Real-time soil monitoring, weather intelligence, and yield prediction systems

In-Person Training Programs

Hands-on agricultural education and capacity building workshops

Physical Resource Provision

Direct supply of essential farming tools and quality seeds

Current Immediate Requirements

Priority Seeds

Soil Enhancement

  • Organic Manure (Primary)
  • NPK Fertilizer (Minimal Use)

Environmental Commitment

A key aspect of our project is promoting sustainable farming practices. While we provide NPK fertilizer when necessary, we emphasize minimal usage and prioritize organic soil enhancement methods to protect both farm soil health and the broader environment.
