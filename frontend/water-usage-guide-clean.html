Quick Reference - Water Requirements by Crop

Crop Water Needs Irrigation Frequency Best Time to Water
Tomatoes High (2-3 cm/week) 2-3 times per week Early morning
Maize Medium (2-2.5 cm/week) 2 times per week Early morning
Beans Medium (1.5-2 cm/week) 2 times per week Morning or evening
Leafy Greens High (frequent light watering) Daily or every other day Evening preferred
Sweet Potatoes Medium (1.5-2 cm/week) 1-2 times per week Early morning
Okra Low-Medium (1-1.5 cm/week) 1-2 times per week Morning
